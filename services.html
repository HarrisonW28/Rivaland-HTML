Our services

Get in touch

Consultancy

Riva Land provides expert consultancy services to help you navigate the complex world of land promotion and planning. Our team offers strategic advice on site selection, planning strategy, and development potential assessment.

We work closely with landowners, developers, and local authorities to identify opportunities and overcome challenges in the planning process. Our consultancy services include feasibility studies, planning strategy development, and expert witness services.

Enquire about Consultancy

Acquisition

Our acquisition services help you identify and secure the right land opportunities for your development projects. We have extensive experience in sourcing, evaluating, and acquiring development land across the UK.

We work with landowners to structure deals that benefit all parties, whether through option agreements, conditional contracts, or joint venture arrangements. Our network and market knowledge enable us to identify opportunities before they come to market.

Enquire about Acquisition

Planning

We navigate the complex planning system to secure planning consent and maximize land value for your projects. Our planning team has a proven track record of success in securing planning permissions for residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments.

Our services include planning application preparation and submission, pre-application consultations, planning appeals, and strategic land promotion. We work with a carefully selected team of consultants to ensure the best possible outcome for each project.
